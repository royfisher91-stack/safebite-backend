## Full Description Draft

SafeBite helps shoppers check food products, review known ingredient and allergen information, compare supermarket availability where data is available, and save products for later.

Use SafeBite to scan or search products, review saved products, view favourites, and keep scan or search history if enabled. SafeBite also supports subscription access and planned add-ons such as SafeHome.

SafeBite Core is planned at GBP 5 per month. SafeHome is planned as a separate paid add-on through `safehome_addon`.

SafeBite is not medical, allergy, nutrition, emergency, or professional advice. Product data may be incomplete, delayed, or wrong. Always check the product label and seek professional guidance where needed.

Supermarket prices, promotions, stock status, and availability can change quickly. SafeBite shows retailer information only where approved or controlled data is available, and this information should be treated as guidance, not a guarantee.
