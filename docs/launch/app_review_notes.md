## Subscription Explanation

SafeBite Core subscription is locked at GBP 5 per month.

Billing structure is prepared for:

- App Store in-app purchases: `apple_iap`
- Google Play Billing: `google_play_billing`

Paid access is not granted until provider subscription status is verified. Promo/influencer access may be used for test or campaign access.

## SafeHome Add-on Explanation

SafeHome is a separate paid add-on using `safehome_addon`. It is intended for household product checks and remains separate from SafeBite food safety scoring.

## Product Safety Disclaimer

SafeBite supports product checking and comparison. It does not replace medical, allergy, nutrition, emergency, manufacturer, or professional advice. Users should check product labels and seek professional advice where needed.

## Supermarket Price/Stock Disclaimer

Supermarket prices, stock status, promotions, and availability can change. SafeBite does not guarantee retailer price or stock accuracy.
