## Long Description Draft

SafeBite helps shoppers check food products before they buy or use them. Scan or search a product to view available product details, ingredient and allergen information where known, supermarket offers where available, saved products, favourites, and scan history.

SafeBite is designed for everyday product checking and comparison. It can help users spot products that may need closer label checking, compare supermarket price and stock information, and find alternatives where data is available.

SafeBite Core is planned as a subscription at GBP 5 per month. SafeHome is a separate paid add-on for household product checks via `safehome_addon`.

Important: SafeBite supports product checking and comparison, but does not replace medical, allergy, nutrition, emergency, manufacturer, or other professional advice. Users should always check the product label and seek professional guidance where needed.

Supermarket prices, promotions, stock status, and availability can change. SafeBite shows retailer information only where approved or controlled data is available, and it should be treated as a guide rather than a guarantee.
